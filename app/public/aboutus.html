We Protect Lives

At the Oconee County Fire Department, we work to keep families safe

All of our operations, from training to first aid to education, focusing on protecting lives. This belief is at the cornerstone of our organisation and effect all activities we pursue. It is the benchmark to which all of our decision are made

Technology

We use the latest in fire-fighting technology to keep you safe. From better tools to high flying planes, we are committed to fighting fires wherever they are

We have invested massive amounts of money and personal time into technologies to keep you safe. We have world-class helicopters and equipment to tackle forest fires before they can reach your homes. We are always looking for new innovations in combating fires.

Training

Training is of the utmost importance for our firefighters. Each of them is committed to reaching the peak of excellence in their field

Each of our fire fighters spend 70% of their time on the job in training. This is so, when fires do come, they have sufficient training to resist and combat different fires. We work with the federal government and other agencies to develop new training curriculum to prepare our firefighters.

Community

We are committed to working with and serving our community. It is our highest honor.

We are consistently working with our community partners to raise awareness about the danger fire poses. From kindergarden to senior service centers, from college to the office, we work with all age groups. We hope that, by enaging with different groups of people, we can safeguard our community and protect it from danger.
