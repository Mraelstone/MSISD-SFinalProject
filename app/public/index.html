Hello from Oconee County Fire Department!

We are excited that our students have the opportunity to travel abroad and experience the wonders of a new country while getting course credit. In the past year, over 500 students at IU made the decision to study abroad in places such as South Korea, Thailand, Australia, Wales, Germany, Italy, and many more!

A global business education is required for today’s executives. That is why we offer so many international programs at Kelley. You can earn credit and customize your experience to fit your academic and career goals.

In addition to study abroad, the second-year Global Foundations Core teaches you to analyze and interpret the influences that drive the global economy. Your experiences abroad will enhance and build on the global business fundamentals learned in these classes.
